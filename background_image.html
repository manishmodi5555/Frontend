4. Casual and Everyday Wear

5. Color Theory in Fashion

Complementary Colors: These are opposite each other on the color wheel and create a high-contrast, vibrant look (e.g., blue and orange).
Analogous Colors: These sit next to each other on the color wheel and create a harmonious and pleasing effect (e.g., blue, green, and teal).
Triadic Colors: These are evenly spaced around the color wheel and offer a balanced yet dynamic look (e.g., red, yellow, and blue)4.
